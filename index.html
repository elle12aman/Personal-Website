Hello
Welcome - I'm Aman Gilani
I'm a post-graduate aerospace engineer, researcher, and a dreamer with her heart set on pushing the boundaries of flight and space exploration. My work spans from designing transonic airfoils to building custom CFD solvers, from analyzing aircraft structures to championing safe, inclusive spaces for marginalized communities.
But my story isn't just all about solving engineering equations in MATLAB or persuing the art and science of capturing fluid motion through CFD — it's about resilience, curiosity, and connections. I've been fortunate to learn, build, and grow through challenges that shaped not only my career, but also the way I see the world. Whether I'm solving a complex fluid dynamics problem, mentoring students, or designing a website for a grassroots organization, my goal is the same: to create something that makes life better for the community around me.
Every project I take on, whether in the lab, the classroom, or the community, is driven by a belief that innovation thrives when we combine technical excellence with empathy, humility, and courage. This site is a glimpse into my journey — where science meets passion, and where every step forward is built on curiosity, collaboration, and a quiet determination to make a difference. This website is a window into my journey — not only as an aerospace engineer, but also as a community leader — highlighting the projects and experiences that have fueled my passion for innovation, inclusion, and cultural connection. I hope to inspire and connect with like-minded individuals, fostering a community eager to push the boundaries of what is possible in the skies and beyond.
I built this website to share my work, showcase my skills, and create opportunities to connect with people and organizations who share a vision for innovation and meaningful collaboration.
