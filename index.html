Hello
I am Aman Gilani, an Aerospace Engineer with a bachelor's degree in Engineering. With an unwavering passion for all things aviation and space, I have immersed myself in the world of aerospace, exploring the intricacies of aerodynamics and aeroelasticity.
As a curious and dedicated individual, I have had the privilege of working on a diverse range of projects that delve deep into the principles and complexities of flight. From the very beginning of my academic journey, I have been captivated by the magic of aerodynamics, studying how air interacts with aircraft surfaces to generate lift and control flight. This fascination has driven me to explore various aspects of aerodynamics, from computational simulations to wind tunnel testing, and I continue to seek innovative solutions to enhance aircraft performance and efficiency.
Understanding the behavior of aircraft structures under different flight conditions has been a fascinating challenge, and I have been actively involved in research projects that address aeroelastic phenomena and contribute to the development of safer and more reliable aircraft designs. This website serves as a platform to share my journey as an Aerospace Engineer and to showcase the projects that have shaped my passion for flight. I hope to inspire and connect with like-minded individuals, fostering a community of aerospace enthusiasts eager to push the boundaries of what is possible in the skies and beyond. I created this website to showcase my skills, share my portfolio, and connect with potential employers and collaborators.
